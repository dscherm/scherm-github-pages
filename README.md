## How to Use

### Getting Started
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. The app will load with a random cocktail to get you started

### Searching for Cocktails

#### By Name
1. Click the "By Name" button (default)
2. Type a cocktail name (e.g., "Margarita", "Mojito")
3. Click "Search" or press Enter

#### By Ingredient
1. Click the "By Ingredient" button
2. Type an ingredient (e.g., "vodka", "lime", "mint")
3. Click "Search" or press Enter

#### By Base Liquor
1. Click the "By Base Liquor" button
2. Select from the visual grid of liquor types
3. Results will appear automatically

#### Random Cocktail
1. Click the "Random" button
2. A random cocktail will be displayed immediately

### Viewing Cocktail Details
1. Click on any cocktail card
2. A modal will open showing:
   - Large cocktail image
   - Full ingredient list
   - Preparation instructions
   - Tags for category, alcohol content, and glass type

### Exploring Ingredients
1. In the cocktail detail view, click on any ingredient
2. A new modal will open with two tabs:
   - **Popular Brands**: See recommended brands for that ingredient
   - **Alternatives**: Discover substitute ingredients

### Creating Your Own Cocktail
1. Click the "Create Your Own" tab at the top
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Cocktail Name**: Give your creation a name
   - **Glass Type**: Choose the serving glass
   - **Ingredients**: Add ingredients with measurements
     - Click "+ Add Ingredient" to add more
     - Click "√ó" to remove an ingredient
   - **Instructions**: Describe how to make it
   - **Image URL**: (Optional) Add a link to an image
3. Click "Save My Cocktail"
4. Your cocktail will appear in the "Your Saved Cocktails" section below

### Managing Saved Cocktails
- View all your saved cocktails in the "Create Your Own" tab
- Click on a saved cocktail to view its details
- Delete cocktails using the "Delete This Cocktail" button
